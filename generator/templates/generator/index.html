Chrono Trigger: Jets of Time Randomizer

Jets of Time is an open world randomizer for Chrono Trigger. Players jet through time with the Epoch looking for additional characters and key items to open the way to, and ultimately defeat, Lavos.

Getting Started

Use the online generator to roll up a seed or download the standalone executable from github.

Generate a seed Get the executable

A valid US Chrono Trigger SNES ROM is required.
